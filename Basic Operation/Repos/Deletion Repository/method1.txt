How to delete repository in GitHub?

There are many ways.

In this article, I will talk the simplest way to reach the goal.

Step 1:
Sign in GitHub with your account.

Step 2:
Select your repository.

Step 3:
Click Settings (which is on the right corner).

Step 4:
Select Settings -> General.

Step 5:
Navigate the "Danger Zone" (at the bottom)

Step 6:
Click "Delete the repository" Buttom.

Step 7:
Type the message it show to confirm to delete the repository.

P.S.
NOTE that the action can NOT undo.
i.e. After deleting, it NO longer exists.
