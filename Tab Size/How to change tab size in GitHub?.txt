
-----------------------------------------------
Step 1:
Open GitHub.
Step 2:
In GitHub, in the upper-right corner of any page, click your profile photo, then click Settings.
Step 3:
In the left sidebar, click  Appearance.
Step 4:
Under "Tab size preference", select the drop-down menu and choose your preference.
