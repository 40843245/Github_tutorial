# Objectives
How to rename a codespace for GitHub?

# Method
## method 1 (In GitHub)
Step 1:
Sign in GitHub with your account.

Step 2:
Click "Codespaces" button(at the middle of top menu).

Step 3:
Select your codespace.

Step 4:
Click "rename" button.

Step 5:
Fill in information.

Step 6:
Click "ok" button.
