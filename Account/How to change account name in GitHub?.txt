There are  many ways to change account in GitHub.
-----------------------------------------------
Step 1:
Open GitHub.
Step 2:
In the upper-right corner of any page, click your profile photo, then click Settings.
Step 3:
In the left sidebar, click  Account.
Step 4:
In the "Change username" section, click Change username.
Step 5:
Read the warnings about changing your username. 
If you still want to change your username, click I understand, let's change my username.
Step 6:
Type a new username.
Step 7:
If the username you've chosen is available, click Change my username. 
If the username you've chosen is unavailable, you can try a different username or one of the suggestions you see.
